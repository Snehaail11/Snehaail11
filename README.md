## 📍 About Me

I'm a **Data & Product professional** who loves building practical, user-focused solutions at the intersection of data, product, and AI.

I enjoy understanding how people make decisions and using data to build better products. I'm not a hardcore AI engineer — but I know how to use AI tools (LLMs, APIs, basic ML) to solve real business problems and build features that users actually need.

During my internship, I built an internal data platform where I handled both technical implementation (full-stack development) and product decisions (requirement gathering, feature prioritization).

**What I'm good at:**
- 📊 Working with data (SQL, Python, Pandas, Power BI, Tableau)
- 🎯 Building data-driven products that solve user problems
- 🤝 Bridging the gap between business needs and technical solutions
- 🔧 Using AI practically (APIs, basic ML, automation) — without overcomplicating

**What I'm looking for:** A role where I can work with data, build products, and use AI practically — ideally at a product company or AI-focused company where I can grow my skills.
